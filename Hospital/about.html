ABOUT US

Historical Background

Saint Gabriel Home Health Services is a residential home health agency dedicated to providing in-home care, skilled nursing and personal care for both adult and the pediatric population in the comfort of their own homes.

The concept of Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services' foundation was inspired by, Mr. Gabriel Kwame Greene. Mr. Gabriel Greene experienced extensive health care services for 7 years prior to his death, after a hit-and-run accident, which remains an unsolved case to this date. Subsequently,Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services provides high quality of care, pursuant to the six domains of the Institute of Medicine (IOM), in terms of equity, safety, efficiency, effectiveness, timelines, and patient centeredness, including compliance with applicable State and Federal rules and regulations.

Who we are

Saint Gabriel Healthcare Services aims to reach out to all members of the community irrespective of race, colour or creed. We strive to maintain an integrated and coordinated in-home care service utilizing multidisciplinary approach. In all that we do, our clients and their families are our priority. We understand that needs and circumstances can change so we take a flexible approach in providing care. Our team is diligent in fulfilling our clients requests, following up, and maintaining good communication.

Mission

Our mission is to protect the Health and safety of our clients/ patients and ensure high levels of confidence in our system through good policies and procedures.

Our Vision

To be the preferred choice of healthcare and medical service provision by saving and enriching lives.
